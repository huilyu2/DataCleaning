# Validating email addresses

Positive Examples:
me@example.com
more.with+symbol@foo.tv
john.doe@example.com
disposable.style.email.with+symbol@example.com
other.email-with-dash@example.com

Negative Examples:
john..doe@example.com
john.@example.com
.john@example.com
A@b@c@example.com
me@some.c
